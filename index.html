About Shurobondita

Shurobondita is IISER Tirupati's most vibrant and cherished annual celebration of Saraswati Puja, the festival dedicated to the goddess of wisdom, knowledge, and arts. This magnificent festival is observed on Vasant Panchami, the fifth day of spring, when nature awakens with new possibilities and fresh beginnings.

üé≠ Our Vision

Shurobondita celebrates the convergence of academic excellence and cultural richness. We believe that a truly educated institution nurtures both the intellectual and creative faculties of its students. Through this festival, we honor Saraswati, the embodiment of knowledge, and create a vibrant space where students, faculty, and the broader community come together to celebrate learning, art, music, and scholarship.

üìç About IISER Tirupati

The Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Tirupati is a premier institution dedicated to research and education in science. Located in the sacred city of Tirupati, IISER Tirupati combines world-class academic standards with India's rich cultural heritage. The institute actively promotes cultural events and celebrations that foster a sense of community and belonging among students.

üéØ The Vasant Panchami Connection

Vasant Panchami marks the arrival of spring and is traditionally associated with learning, wisdom, and new beginnings. Celebrated across India with great fervor, this day is dedicated to Saraswati, who represents knowledge, arts, music, and wisdom. Shurobondita harnesses this spirit to create an unforgettable experience for all participants.
